Magnified SMSF SEO & Lead Growth Audit

Priority technical, content, local SEO and Google Ads recommendations for magnifiedsmsf.com.au.

Prepared from site crawl, Google Search Console data, keyword research and competitor checks.

Executive summary

Magnified SMSF is in a decent technical position, but it is not yet strong enough for the main Perth SMSF commercial keywords. The fastest path to more enquiries is to fix domain consolidation, strengthen the Perth and Mandurah landing pages, add more “SMSF accountant” language where accurate, and run tightly controlled Google Ads campaigns with call/form tracking.

Top technical issue: https://www.magnifiedsmsf.com.au/ currently resolves separately instead of 301 redirecting to https://magnifiedsmsf.com.au/. Fixing this should be first.

Competitor patterns

Competitors found directionally include SMSF Australia, SMSF Services Perth, WA SMSF Specialists, Pine SMSF, Greenwood Accountants Perth, Armada and Blueprint Wealth.

  • SMSF Australia has a long exact-match “SMSF Accountants Perth” page.
  • SMSF Services Perth benefits from an exact-match domain/brand and uses “SMSF Accountant Perth”, “SMSF Consultants Perth” and “SMSF Specialist Advisor”.
  • WA SMSF Specialists uses strong proof: Perth, Western Australia, trusted by 200+ clients, 20 years.
  • Pine SMSF has a direct value proposition: “Perth’s complete end to end SMSF experts”.
  • Greenwood has exact “SMSF Accountants Perth” H1/title and accounting/tax alignment.
  • Blueprint has broader “Self Managed Super Funds Perth - SMSF Advice” coverage and a quiz conversion asset.

Priority action plan

Critical1. Fix canonical/domain consolidation

  • 301 redirect all www URLs to the non-www version.
  • Keep canonical tags pointing to non-www.
  • Redirect HTTP to HTTPS non-www.
  • Monitor GSC as the duplicate www homepage consolidates.

Critical2. Redirect old PDF and WordPress URLs

  • Choose one canonical SMSF guide PDF or, ideally, a dedicated HTML guide landing page.
  • Redirect old /wp-content/uploads/... PDF URLs to the current guide or guide page.
  • Add the guide page to the sitemap if it is intended to rank/capture leads.

High3. Optimise the Perth money page

Target /perth-smsf-specialists/ around “SMSF accountant Perth”, “SMSF accountants Perth”, “self managed super fund Perth”, “SMSF Perth” and “SMSF specialist Perth”.

Suggested title: SMSF Accountants Perth | SMSF Specialists WA

Suggested H1: SMSF Accountants & Specialists in Perth

  • Add sections for setup, administration, annual returns, compliance, tax planning, pension phase, investment strategy and why use a specialist SMSF accountant.
  • Add Mandurah-based/serving Perth online or in person positioning.
  • Add CPA Practice, SAN AFSL credentials, testimonials and FAQs.

High4. Build local “SMSF accountant near me” intent

  • Use Google Business Profile, reviews, NAP consistency, embedded map, directions and suburb mentions.
  • Strengthen Mandurah and Perth landing pages rather than over-building thin suburb pages.

High5. Strengthen Mandurah local dominance

  • Add “SMSF Accountant Mandurah” language.
  • Add address detail: Unit 2/106 Pinjarra Road, Mandurah WA 6210.
  • Add relevant local areas such as Halls Head, Greenfields, Meadow Springs, Falcon, Erskine, Lakelands, Pinjarra and Peel if accurate.
  • Add parking/visit info, appointment types, local FAQ and LocalBusiness schema.

High6. Improve setup and compliance pages

  • Add “SMSF registration” content to the setup/admin page.
  • Explain trust deed, corporate trustee, ABN/TFN, ATO registration, bank account, rollover, investment strategy and annual obligations.
  • Retitle compliance page toward “SMSF Compliance Specialists Mandurah & Perth”.
  • Add sections for annual SMSF return, financial statements, ATO compliance, audit support, records, contributions, pensions and Class Super reporting.

Medium7. Add schema and on-page cleanup

  • Add LocalBusiness / FinancialService, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, Article and Person schema where relevant.
  • Shorten long blog titles and service titles.
  • Improve short meta descriptions.
  • Add missing image alt text.
  • Remove, noindex or redirect the “Coming Soon” page if it has no purpose.

Medium8. Build internal links and content cluster

  • Link blog posts to service pages with anchors like “SMSF annual return”, “SMSF investment strategy”, “SMSF setup”, “SMSF tax planning” and “SMSF records”.
  • Add “Need help with this?” CTA boxes inside blog posts.
